Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Wealth Proxies.
What is the delivery time after purchase?

Residential proxies are delivered instantly and appear on your dashboard within seconds. ISP proxies are usually fulfilled instantly as well, but in rare cases may require manual provisioning. If that happens, delivery will be completed within 24 hours at most.

 What payment methods do you accept?

We use Stripe as our payment processor, which supports credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Link, and other major payment methods.

 Do you offer free trials?

We don’t currently offer free trials for standard B2C purchases. However, you can start with a small quantity to test performance. If you're interested in bulk or B2B plans, please contact support to discuss trial options.

 What is the customer support availability & response time?

Our team actively monitors support tickets via Discord and live chat. While we don’t guarantee 24/7 coverage, we aim to respond to all inquiries promptly and efficiently.

 Cam I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely. You can change your plan at any time through your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.
